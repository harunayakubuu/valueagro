Alhaji Abubakar Usman

Chairman

Value Agro Allied.

The Chairman, Alhaji Abubakar Usman is an experienced entreprenuer with innovative business ideas encircled with competent team of experts from different fields. He has experience in import and export, logistics, input and agricultural commodities with long-term working relationship with Fertilizer Companies (Stallion group, Indorama, Notore, and Dangote).
He initiated and lead the following companies; Abadan Seed Limited, Abadan Real Estate Developers Limited, Abadan Atlantic, Value Agro Allied Unique Synergy System Limited, A U Sambo De-trading Co Limited, Sambo standard Vision Limited, Doman Aged Logistic services Limited, Belcom Standard Investment Limited, Qadar Oil and Petroleum Limited as well as Spectrum Continental Synergy Limited. In supporting the nation to develop effective structures to deliver services to the people, Alhaji Abubakar was involved in Logistics with Celulant Limited, Agro Dealership, Transporting, and commodity marketing. Alhaji Abubakar Usman hailed from North-Eastern Nigeria but has track records of philanthropism across the length and breadth of the country. A well-travelled young executive with lots of international contacts and a huge base of local connections.
